Scratch Day Taiwan

Organizer and Co-host | May 2019 - Present

Overview

We have been organizing annual Scratch Day events and workshops in Taiwan since 2019! Each summer, we partner with high school student volunteers to deliver a day of free workshops and activities for kids in elementary school! It's a great way to introduce Scratch to more people as well as share the joy of teaching with the volunteering students!
