Scratch Day Taiwan

Organizer and Co-host | May 2019 - Present

Overview

Scratch Day Taiwan is an annual event we host for free, for kids of all ages in Taiwan. We usually host a day of workshops during the first weekend of summer break and make fun Scratch projects with the kids and parents!

Past Scratch Day Events

Scratch Day TW 2019

May 18, 2019
The first ever Scratch Day Taiwan I organized! This was my first time teaching for a live audience and I was extremely nervous to deliver the workshop back then! I can't recall too many details, all I remember was how surprised I felt when my friends were so quick to say yes when I asked them to come help out as volunteers. This year taught me that people are generally willing to help out out of curiosity. Sometimes all it takes is a warm and enthusiastic invitation!

Scratch Day TW 2020

July 18, 2020
I still think this is one of our most successful Scratch Day events to date! We had a great venue and such a wonderful turn out! This was also the year I published my book so that might be skweing my rose tinted view a certain way. I think the most memorable thing this year was how well the simple yet modular maze game workshop lesson plan I devised worked. I could see that kids of all experience ranges could find something engaging to latch on to and ultimately a simple engaging game was much more effective than some of the more theoretical or experimental workshops I've tried delivering in the following years.

Scratch Day TW 2022

July 16, 2022
(If you're wondering where Scratch Day Taiwan 2021 went we had to cancel it due to the pandemic.) In 2022 we had a smaller event since regular in-person events were just slowly returning, after all the lockdowns. This year really showed us the importance of a good and easy to find venue!

Scratch Day TW 2023

July 8, 2023
This was the first year I started experimenting with more experimental workshop content for the event. ChatGPT had gotten really popular around that time and I wanted to try something involving the new tech. The workshop was titled "Little AI Director" and we gave the kids a fill in the blanks activity which they later put into AI as a prompt for a story. As all first experiments go, this one left me with much improvement to be desired. However, I did see first hand how including marketable ideas dramatically increases interest in our little event. This is something I am still learning to balance: catering to parents in the marketing, catering to children in the actual classroom.

Scratch Day TW 2024

July 6, 2024
Our second year collaborating with Dayuan International Senior High School, a local school in our area. We are grateful for the continuous support from the teachers and student volunteers each year. This year the theme was making a "Forest Fairy" inspired by Koroks from the Legend of Zelda!

Scratch Day TW 2025

July 5, 2025
This year we were once again working with Dayuan International Senior High school. We are working on involving the high school volunteers more and more each year. The hope is with more involvement from the volunteers we will be able to improve our capacity and reach larger groups more effectively! This year also got me thinking about the longer term plan for Scratch Day Taiwan as a whole. Since this is something I've committed to organizing each year, I think it would be great if there could be some continuous progression that can accumulate year on year... Maybe it will be in the form of developing a reusable modular lesson plan that is both engaging, practical, and fun!
